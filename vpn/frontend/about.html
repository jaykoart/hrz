Frequently Asked Questions

Does Horizon see my browsing history?

Never. We operate on a strict Zero-Logs basis. Technically, our nodes cannot inspect the encrypted traffic inside your personal VPN tunnel. Your private browsing remains private.

Will it slow down my internet connection?

Imperceptibly. The background process is 'Resource-Aware'. It only activates when your system is idle (e.g., you are away from the computer) and strictly limits bandwidth usage to ensure your streaming or gaming is never interrupted.

Is it really free forever?

Yes. We don't need your subscription money because our business model is built on B2B data intelligence, not B2C software sales. As long as you contribute to the network, the premium service is yours.

Can my IP be used for illegal activities?

No. Unlike other P2P networks, Horizon separates the "Commercial Proxy" (used by businesses) from your residential IP. We have strict KYC (Know Your Customer) vetting for all business partners accessing the data network.

Engineering Deep Dive

Transparency
Report

I. RAM-Only Infrastructure Traditional VPNs write logs to hard drives, which can be seized or inspected. Horizon operates exclusively on RAM-Only Servers. This means that the moment a server is rebooted—or if power is cut—every single byte of data is instantly wiped. There is no physical disk to recover data from. We treat your browsing history as "Toxic Data" that we refuse to store.

II. The 'Blind' Relay Architecture We utilize a "Double-Hop" architecture for the initial handshake. The node that knows who you are (the entry node) does not know what you are doing. The node that knows what you are doing (the exit node) does not know who you are. This cryptographic separation ensures that no single machine in our network possesses the complete picture of your activity.

III. Ephemeral Keys & Forward Secrecy Horizon uses Perfect Forward Secrecy (PFS). We generate a new encryption key for every single session, and rotate them every 60 minutes. Even if a supercomputer were to crack a key from a past session (which would take billions of years), it could not be used to decrypt any future or past sessions. Your privacy is mathematically guaranteed, not just promised.

IV. The Business Firewall Critics ask: "If you sell data, isn't my data at risk?" No. The "Commercial Proxy" network is a completely separate logical layer. Business partners access the public internet through our nodes to check flight prices or ad placements. They never have access to the internal VPN tunnel where your personal traffic flows. The two worlds never touch.
